HOW TO CHOOSE A PERFECT LAPTOP ?
Choosing ideal processor for your laptop-

U series , H series , HX series

u series (For professional and basic coding) are known for the least power consumption and least performance , thus they are used for sustaining the power for long time .

h series (For professional , good level coding , basic video editing , basic gaming) they use a moderate amount of power and delivers much more power to laptop compared to U series . So they are the most used processor in market .

hx series are the most powerful processor used in laptops they consume a good amount of power and thus delivers a significant amount of power to laptop making it the most powerful processor in laptops.
Ram ( RANDOM ACCESS MEMORY )
ram are essential component of computer as it defines how much work we can do with a laptop at same time seamlessly . In 2024 ddr4 , ddr5 ram are mainly used in laptops as they are powerful and capable of doing most of the tasks . NOTE - ddr3 rams in 2024 are not recomended .
STORAGE TYPE
Storage type plays an important role in selection of laptop.

M.2 NVMe SSD( GEN 2 , GEN 3 , GEN 4 , GEN 5 )

In 2024 M.2 NVMe SSD of GEN 3 , GEN 4 and GEN 5 are mostly used in laptops , because ssds play a important role in storing of every information , they are also used in al the data transfers in the laptop . The more fast the ssd the most fast the data processing ( sending + recieving ) speed .
USE OF GPUs IN LAPTOP
rtx20series , rtx 30series , rtx 40 series
Gpu's are the most power consuming components of laptops . Thus laptops having powerful gps have a batery life of about 2-3 hours. They are useful for advance level video editing ( using many layers ),advance level gaming , making MACHINE LEARNING models . 20series is a mid range gpu can be found in laptops around 50,000 to 60,000 . 30series is a mid to high range gpu can be found in laptops around 60,000 to 80,000 . 40series are high level gpu used in high graphics and power demanding tasks . *TGP matters.
